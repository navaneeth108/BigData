Q25. What is the difference between .loc and .iloc in Pandas?

 

 

both of them helps in the convenient selection of data from the DataFrame in Python

loc is label-based selection.

 

df1.loc['name']

 

 

iloc is integer position-based,

 

df1.iloc[0]
